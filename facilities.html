Facilities

Presentation Equipment

All meeting rooms are equipped with HDMI projectors.

Speakers should connect their laptops to the projector via HDMI.

You do not need to bring an HDMI cable. However, if your laptop does not have an HDMI port, please bring an HDMI adapter.

VGA connection is also available. In that case, you do not need to bring a VGA cable, but please bring a VGA adapter.

Wi-Fi

If you have an Eduroam account, you can use Wi-Fi throughout the campus.
