From Bus Stop to Tenchikan Building

  1. There is a long escalator in front of the university bus stop.

  2. Take this escalator up.

  3. Once you reach the top of the escalator, walk left along the sidewalk.

  4. You will see another escalator.

  5. Take this second escalator up. You will then see the Faculty of Science building (Banyukan). Turn left just before it.

  6. You will see the Tenchikan building.

  7. You have arrived at Tenchikan.
