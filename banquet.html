Banquet

Location and Time

The banquet will take place at Ganko Takasegawa Nijoen (Japanese cuisine restaurant) on Wed, Sep. 10 at 18:00pm.

The excursion bus will directly go there.

If you come to the banquet by yourself, please see the location: https://maps.app.goo.gl/Xr1DKVw8wdyUV6Ea8.

Notes

Please do not forget to bring your name tag and banquet ticket.

For vegetarians and those with dietary restrictions who have informed us in advance, there is a mark on the name tag and ticket. Please have the mark checked at the reception.
