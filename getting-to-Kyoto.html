Getting to Kyoto

! This page is under construction. !

From Kansai Airport (KIX)

  • Route 1: (easy and fast)

    Kansai Airport

    The JR Haruka limited express (approx. 1h 10min)

    JR Kyoto Station

  • Route 2: (cheap)

    Kansai Airport

    JR Kansaikuko Line Kanku-Rapid (approx. 1h 10min)

    JR Osaka Station (transfer)

    JR Kyoto Line Special Rapid (approx. 30min)

    JR Kyoto Station

  • Route 3: (easy)

    Kansai Airport

    Kansai Airport Limousine (approx. 1h 30min)

    Kyoto Sta. Hachijo-guchi

From Kobe Airport (UKB)

  • Route:

    Kobe Airport

    Portliner (Kobe New Transit railway) (< 20min)

    Sannomiya Sta. (Kobe New Transit)

    Transfer (5min walk)

    JR Sannomiya Sta.

    JR Special Rapid (approx. 50min)

    JR Kyoto Station

    This route is easy and sufficiently fast. So Shinkansen is not recommended.

From Narita Airport (NRT)

  • Route 1:

    Narita Airport

    Narita Express (railway, approx. 1 hour)

    JR Tokyo Sta. (transfer)

    Shinkansen Nozomi (approx. 2h 15min)

    JR Kyoto Station

  • Route 2:

    Narita Airport

    Skyliner (Limited Express Train)(approx. 40+min)

    Nippori Sta. (transfer)

    JR Yamanote Line Toward Ueno (approx. 10+min)

    JR Tokyo Sta. (transfer)

    Shinkansen Nozomi (approx. 2h 15min)

    JR Kyoto Station

From Haneda Airport (HND)

  • Route:

    Haneda Airport

    Keikyu-Kuko Line Limited Express (railway, approx. 20min)

    Shinagawa Sta. (transfer)

    Shinkansen Nozomi (approx. 2h 05min)

    JR Kyoto Station
