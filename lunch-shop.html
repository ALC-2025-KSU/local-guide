Lunch Information

There are few restaurants and shops near the university. Also, only two restaurants on campus are open due to summer vacation. The capacity of these restaurants is also limited.

So, if you attend the conference for the whole period, we strongly recommend you to choose the registration option with lunch boxes for five days. (Registration is now closed.)

If you ordered lunch boxes during registration...

You can pick up your lunch boxes at the following locations:

  • From Sep. 8 (Mon.) to Sep. 11 (Thu.): Tenchikan Lounge on the 1st floor
  • On Sep. 12 (Fri.): 2nd floor of Heirakukan (Campus Map)

Please hand your ticket to the staff or show your name tag before taking your lunch box.

Choose one from the following three types of lunch boxes:

  • Lunch box 1: Uzumasa (Allergy info: egg, flour, squid, mackerel, chicken, soybeans, salmon, sesame)
  • Lunch box 2: Beef rice (Allergy info: flour, beef, soybeans)
  • Lunch box 3: Saijiki (Allergy info: egg, flour, mackerel, soybeans, salmon, sesame)

They are available on a first-come, first-served basis.

Vegetarian lunch boxes will be prepared for those who indicated so during registration. Your lunch ticket and name tag have a mark indicating that you applied for a vegetarian lunch box.

If you didn't order lunch boxes...

The eating places that will be open in the KSU campus during the conference are the following:

  1. Sukiya (beef bowl restaurant)
  2. Ichibariki (ramen restaurant)
  3. FamilyMart (convenience store)
