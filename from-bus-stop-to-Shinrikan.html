— ALC 2025 Local Guide —

From Bus Stop to Shinrikan Building.

  1. There is a long escalator in front of the university bus stop.

  2. Take this escalator up.

  3. Once you reach the top of the escalator, walk left along the sidewalk.

  4. You will see another escalator. Turn right just before it. (Do not take the escalator.)

  5. Continue walking for a while.

  6. You will then see a building on your left, like the one in the photo below. This is Building #12.
    Continue walking until you reach the pilotis of Building #12.

  7. Go through the pilotis.

  8. Once you have gone through the pilotis, go right.

  9. You will see the Shinrikan building. The conference rooms SR401〜SR404 are on the 4th floor.
