From Kyoto Sta. via Kita-Oji

This is the simplest route and is recommended.

Kyoto Sta. (Platform 2)

Subway Karasuma Line (For Kokusaikaikan) (14min)

Kita-Oji Sta.

Transfer (1min)

Kita Oji Bus Terminal

Bus Line "North 3" (approx. 20min)

Kyoto Sangyo University (City Bus)

The bus departs from stop "A" on the RED Platform.

From Kyoto Sta. via Kokusaikaikan

Kyoto Sta. (Platform 2)

Subway Karasuma Line (For Kokusaikaikan) (20min)

Kokusaikaikan Sta.

Transfer (1min)

Kokusai-Kaikan Station (Kyoto Bus)

Bus Line #40 (approx. 11min)

Kyoto Sangyo University (Kyoto Bus)

From Demachiyanagi Sta.

This route is not recommended, but if you're staying somewhere along the Keihan line, it might be worth considering, because it's easy to transfer from the Keihan line to the Eizan line.

Demachiyanagi Sta.

Eizan Railway (For Kurama) (approx. 20min)

Nikenchaya Sta.

Walk (approx. 12min)

Kyoto Sangyo University

※ The bus route is omitted here because the Kyoto Bus route from Demachiyanagi to KSU has very few buses during this season.

Taxi from Kita-Oji Sta.

If you take a taxi from Kita-Oji Station to KSU, it will cost around 2,000 yen and take about 10 minutes.

There is a taxi stand at Kitaoji Station and there are always taxis waiting, so it's easy to catch a taxi.
